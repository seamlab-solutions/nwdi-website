The New World Diagnostics, Inc. was established in 1996 to fulfill the idea of building a medical laboratory to serve the Filipino community, and to advance the philosophy of helping the public achieve better health. The company was originally registered as Prime Physicians Health Maintenance, Inc. (PPHMI) under the Securities and Exchange Commission.
