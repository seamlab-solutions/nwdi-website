PATIENT LOYALTY CARD

What is the PLC?

The Patient Loyalty Card (PLC) Program is an identification and a reward card for patients of New World Diagnostics and Premium Medical. It contains a unique patient identification number that will facilitate faster transactions. The card will also earn points per transaction; earned points can be used to pay for services at any New World Diagnostics branches and Premium Medical Clinic.

Benefits of the PLC to patients:

  • Faster Patient Identification and transaction
    The PLC serves as the patient’s lifetime Identification Card at NWDI and Premium Medical. The patient will no longer be required to fill-up the Patient Data Form and will just present the PLC and Physician's Request to the Receptionist in order to be accommodated.

  • Points Earner
    Every One Hundred Pesos (Php100) worth of paid services is equivalent to One (1) point.

Terms and Conditions

Upon receipt of the card, the patient is required to sign at the space provided on the back of the card. By signing, the patient agrees to the terms and conditions, guidelines and rules of Membership.

  • Validity
    The card: The PLC contains a unique Patient Identification Number which shall be the same during the lifetime of the Member. There is no need for renewal as the card entitles the Member to lifetime membership.

  • Use of the card

    1. The PLC can be used at all NWDI clinics and Premium Medical.
    2. PLC and the Points earned is non-transferrable.
    3. PLC is required for Points Redemption.

Member/Patient Information

  1. Member/Patient Information is based on the Information Sheet the patient has filled-up upon registration. Any changes in the initial information, Member may request for updating of record.
  2. The Member agrees to receive communications, including but not limited to electronic and postal materials from NWDI. Such materials may be in the form of programs updates, exclusive promotions and tie-up offers that NWDI deems beneficial to the Member.

Earning of Points:

  1. Every paid transaction will entitle the Member to earn an equivalent Point.
  2. Points from transactions shall be credited upon payment while Points earned from cancelled transactions shall likewise be deducted.

Redemption of Points:

  1. Any transaction may be paid for by using the earned points at prevailing conversion rates.
  2. The Points cannot be converted to cash.
  3. For the redemption of Points, those Points reflected in NWDI records shall be deemed correct and NWDI has the exclusive right to determine the available amount of Points to be awarded to or redeemed by the Member.
  4. Redeemed Points or Privileges are not exchangeable for other Awards or Privileges, or refundable, replaceable, or be sold or transferred for cash or credit, under any circumstances.
  5. By redeeming Points for Award or Privileges, the Member releases NWDI and its Partners from any and all liability to the Member regarding the use and redemption of Points and on the issuance and usage of said Awards or Privileges. Points redeemed may no longer be changed, revoked, or cancelled.

Lost or Damaged Card

  1. Re-issuance fee of One Hundred Fifty Pesos (Php150.00) is required for card replacement.

Cancellation or Termination

  1. Any breach of Terms and Conditions whether intentional or otherwise may result in termination of Membership, forfeiture of issued PLC, and cancellation of Points, Awards or Privileges at the sole discretion of NWDI. Eligibility for future or further participation in the PLC Program shall likewise be affected.
  2. The following constitutes a breach of the Terms and Conditions by the Member:
    1. To abuse any awards, privileges, facilities, services or arrangements accorded to the Member as a result of Membership in the PLC program
    2. To act in any fraudulent manner or in any way which is likely to be detrimental to the interest of NWDI or its Partners
    3. To provide or attempt to provide misleading information, or make any misrepresentation to NWDI or its Partners;
    4. To act in any way which in NWDI’s reasonable opinion breaches or is likely to breach these Terms and Condition.
  3. NWDI’s decision as to what constitutes a breach shall be final and conclusive.
  4. In the event of termination, all Points, Awards or Privileges that may have been earned or accrued by the Member will also be terminated, and these may not be sold, assigned or transferred or otherwise redeemed. NWDI and its Partners shall in no way be liable for any Points, Awards or Privileges of a terminated Member.

Other conditions:

  1. The PLC is and shall remain the property of NWDI and must be returned on demand if there is a determination of abuse on the part of the Member.
  2. NWDI shall use reasonable means to provide the Members with accurate and timely information regarding the PLC including any changes thereof; however, NWDI and its Partners shall not be liable in any way for failure to do so.
